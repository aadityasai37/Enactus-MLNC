Enactus Worldwide

Established in 1975, Enactus (formerly known as Students In Free Enterprise) is an international, non-political, non-profit international organization that tively brings students, business and academic leaders together by channelling the power of entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of life and standard of living of those in need. Today, Enactus has its presence in more than 36 countries, engaging students from over 1700 colleges. Through the community outreach projects undertaken by the students, nearly 6 lakh lives have been impacted in India alone.

About Us

Enactus was established in MLNC(Day) in the year 2014.
Since then, driven by commitment, integrity, and innovation, our team has focused on tackling social problems to improve and revamp lives of the underprivileged and the stray dogs through various projects.
Till now, we have worked on 5 projects, namely, Parivartan, Desi, Sneh, Kawach, Prabhakshay. Out of these, Kawach has been successfully completed in the month of October, 2020 !
